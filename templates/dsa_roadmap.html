📋 Daily Practice Schedule

🌅 Morning (1 hr)
  • Review yesterday's solved problems
  • Read theory for today's topic (GeeksforGeeks)
  • Watch 1 explanation video (NeetCode / Abdul Bari)
☀️ Afternoon (2 hrs)
  • Solve 1 Easy LeetCode (warm-up)
  • Solve 1-2 Medium LeetCode problems
  • Write code in preferred language (Python/Java/C++)
🌙 Evening (1 hr)
  • Review solutions you couldn't solve
  • Note patterns and approaches in a notebook
  • Plan tomorrow's topic

💡 Pro Tips

Don't look at the solution for at least 45 minutes
After solving, always check if your solution is optimal
Practice explaining your approach out loud (mock interview prep)
Track solved problems in a spreadsheet — patterns become clear
Consistency > intensity. 2 hours/day beats 8-hour cramming
